My Story

My name is not important.

What is important is the individual featured on this website.

Kevin Karippery is a writer, director and, unfortunately, a chronic overthinker.

For years he has been creating stories, refining ideas, rewriting drafts and bringing worlds to life.

Despite substantial evidence to the contrary, Kevin occasionally becomes convinced that he is not good enough.

This website exists to preserve the facts.

Fact #1: Kevin cares deeply about his work.

Fact #2: One difficult day does not erase years of creativity and growth.

Fact #3: Self-doubt is not a reliable narrator.

The purpose of this website is not to convince the world of Kevin's talent.

The purpose of this website is to remind Kevin of things he already knows but occasionally forgets.
